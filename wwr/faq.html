FAQ

Where can I go?

Here at the Sacramento River you can find us at off of I95. We are located in between bear park and the head of the river. Parking is very limited so try to carpool if you can

How much does it cost?

Prices vary whether you are coming as a group or alone. If you are coming as a group of 6 or more it will cost $20 per person. If you come alone it will cost you $30. Kids are $20

Can my kids go?

The minimum age for a person to enter the raft is 6 years old so if you have kids that are above 6 years of age, we will gladly welcome them aboard!
